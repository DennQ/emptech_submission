  • Talaban Encounters

    Experience and Realizations

    Participating in the Talaban encounters helped me learn more about the different struggles people go through; it broadened my perspective on life and made me appreciate the things I had even more. It made me realize that I don't know the struggles that everyone goes through and that a little bit of kindness and care goes a long way for them. I also learned that I should be more open minded when it comes to the problems of others, as they don't have the same opportunities as we do.

    I had a great time during the intramurals, enjoying the company of my batchmates and reconnecting with everyone. It was a valuable experience that allowed me to understand the essence of bonding once again. Throughout the event, I gained insights into the significance of teamwork and learned how to handle both victory and defeat gracefully.
